FAQ

Straight answers.

The questions every owner asks before the first call.

What does custom software cost?

The audit and the scope are free. Once we agree on a plan, you pay to build it, then a flat monthly rate covers hosting, support, and updates. You don't pay anything until you agree to the plan, and the final payment isn't due until it's live and working the way we said it would.

How long until it's live?

Days to weeks, not months. Most projects go from first call to live product in under two weeks. We build around your workflow instead of sitting in planning meetings.

What happens on the first call?

We find what's actually costing you time and money, then tell you what we'd build to fix it. About 20 minutes, no packages, no pressure. If there's nothing worth doing, we'll say so.

Who owns and runs it after it's built?

We run it for you on a flat monthly rate: hosting, updates, and support included. Month-to-month, no long contracts — it's built for your business and stays yours. Something breaks after hours, we handle it.

Is my customer data safe?

Yes. Our AI runs on our own hardware, not OpenAI and not Google. Your customers' data never leaves our control and isn't sent to a third-party cloud.

Do you only work with shops in Temple?

No. The shop management system is one thing we've shipped, but we build custom software and AI automation for small businesses across Central Texas — Temple, Belton, Killeen, Waco, and the greater Austin area.
